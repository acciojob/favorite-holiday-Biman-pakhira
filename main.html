Ladakh


Ladakh is a mountainous region in India known as the "land of high mountain passes", featuring high-altitude deserts, rugged mountains, deep valleys, and breathtaking landscapes. Situated in the Himalayas and influenced by Tibetan-Buddhist culture, Ladakh is famous for its monasteries, crystal-clear lakes like Pangong Tso, and thrilling adventure activities.
